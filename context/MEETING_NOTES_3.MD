# AI-powered English learning app prototype development for Japanese students

Thu, 12 Mar 26

### Product Demo & Feedback

- Presented English learning app prototype built in 1 week
  - Mobile-friendly daily practice exercises
  - Progress tracking with fillable slots
  - Questions pulled from existing lesson database
  - Basic foundation ready for AI integration
- Team impressed with rapid development timeline
- App serves as blueprint for future enhancements

### Value Proposition Concerns

- Current version too basic - needs clearer value differentiation
- Key challenge: What unique value does this add beyond existing touchpoints?
- Japanese student characteristics to address:
  1. Very shy and embarrassed when speaking
  2. Reluctant to make mistakes
  3. Limited English practice opportunities
  4. Need confidence building before human interaction
- Suggested approach: Add onboarding question about specific prep needs
  - Options: speaking to people, talking about self, etc.

### AI Integration Opportunities

- Production/conversation practice currently nerve-wracking for students
  - AI could help prepare students for human conversations
  - Reduce anxiety before speaking with tutors
- Need to determine optimal AI vs human value split
- Current lessons focus on lesson plans vs actual conversation
  - App could automate repetitive teaching elements
  - Free up human tutors for real conversation practice

### Content Quality Control Challenge

- Major concern: AI-generated content reliability
- Currently manually checking 7,000 sentences for appropriateness
- Question: Can AI check AI-generated content effectively?
  - Why do inappropriate words still appear despite filters?
  - What prevents better automated content moderation?
- Team struggling with this across all AI implementations

### Data Collection Potential

- App will generate valuable usage data
  - Correct/incorrect answer patterns
  - Commonly missed words and sentence types
  - Student performance analytics
- Could inform AI model development
- Potential integration with tutor platform to show student prep data

### Next Steps

- Team to develop enhanced version with AI features for next meeting
- Focus on adding clear value proposition beyond basic functionality
- Explore creative AI applications for English learning
- Schedule follow-up meeting (Malampa to coordinate)
